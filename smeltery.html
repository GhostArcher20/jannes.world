The Smeltery

Here in the smeltery you can use the ores you mined in the mine to turn them into ingots.
You will have to put the ore into the furnace to turn it into a bloom first though!.
The bloom has to be worked to turn into a propper ingot, you can do this by hitting it with a certain strength.
make sure to work it while it's hot enough, the hotter the more efficient your hits will be.
if the bloom gets too cold you will have to reheat it.

make sur not to hit it too many times though, because otherwise it will be messed up and you will lose some material.
