I am depressed. I have been depressed for a very long time. Longer than I can remember. One of my early memories of childhood was my mom asking me if I was okay, asking if I needed anything (small sidenote, I am very lucky with my family, they are kind and thoughtfull and I've never lacked anything, except one thing maybe) when she asked me that, I had been feeling like shit already for a long time, and I just answered "nothing", I didn't wanna do anything, I didnt need anything, I just wanted to disappear. Now I am 26 years old, still depressed, I still want to disappear. I'll try to write a bit on how I feel, and maybe why I feel this. I am sad, tired, disappointed, angry, scared, and hopeless. I am unemployed even though I've been trying to get a job. I only just graduated in July of last year. I don't have a drivers licence I'm not very smart and I really dislike how I look. My girlfriend cheated on me in July of last year when we were long distance. Now we live together and I finally figured it out on the 3rd of january of this year. I stayed with her because I love her. I'm not sure if she loves me, but for now that is good enough. I have been trying to write this article to get it published. But I am useless and I can't manage to finish it. The promoter of my thesis asked me if I wanted to try to get funding for a phd. I finally said yes. That means that there are constantly 3 things on my mind that I can't seem to solve. Finding a job, getting my driver's licence and writing my article. Sometimes I manage to forget that she cheated on me. But then it just comes back. Sometimes I blame her, sometimes I blame myself. When I'm sad, she says things she means. like "we can't go on like this" or "if I get the feeling you only want us temporarily, we're gone". She's not a mean person. She's actually really kind and thoughtful and sweet. Her past hasn't been easy and I understand her fear of abandonment. But sometimes it really hurts when she says something like that. Today I was just eating lunch, and suddenly I felt tears swell in my eyes. I tried to stop it and think of something to stop myself from crying. But of course she notices and took my arm. I couldn't stop it but I really didn't want to. I started sobbing so I left. I started doing the laundry to stop crying. I stopped now but I feel like it could come out any second. That's why I'm writing, sometimes it helps me to get my feelings out. People always say it's okay to cry. Like yeah of course it's okay, but that doesn't make it fun. I don't like crying. the headache, the stuffy nose, the stingy eyes. There's nothing good about crying. "But you feel relieved afterwards". what? I have never once felt relieved after crying. At best nothing changed about the situation except that now you have a headache and trouble thinking, at worst people are making fun of you or hold you in a different light entirely. Sure crying is natural and if it helps some people, perfect. But Don't tell me I should cry more, I hate it, I cry too much as it is. Anyway. The state of the world is also not doing my depression any good. The prospect of never owning anything in my life is not exactly inspiring to live. And then there's everything else in the world that is also completely fucked. TL;DR: I want to die.
