Maakdag: boezdaalhoeve

My girlfriend and I went to the "maakdag" (Dutch for "make day"). It's a place where you can do all kinds of diy projects, the material is provided and there's always a passionate volunteer that organized a certain arts & crafts project. We went to two of them (that's all we had time for), the first one was dry-felting, the second was wood-turning.

Dry felting

It was my first time trying this so I didn't really know what was hard to do or not. At first I thought it was gonna be super hard, so I just wanted to do different colors, but then I kind of wanted to do a branch with some leaves.

The volunteer helping us had also provided a whole bag of excess wool that was used in other projects, of course these pieces weren't one singular color (like the other material she had provided), but I really liked that some pieces had different colors within them. I didn't know how it was going to turn out when I found a dark green piece with pink strands, but I really like it actually.

Then I finished up with a little red bird, and although I didn't have high hopes, I honestly think it's pretty cute! So if you ever have the opportunity, try your hand at some (dry-)felting :).
